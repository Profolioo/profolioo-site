Who it's for

One platform, every role in the classroom

Profolioo adapts to how you actually work — whether you're sharing work, reviewing growth, or running an entire institution.

🎓

For Students

Build a living record of your academic journey. Upload projects, reflect on your growth, and share polished portfolios with anyone — colleges, employers, or just your family.

  • Drag-and-drop portfolio builder
  • Multimedia project uploads (video, PDF, images)
  • Personal reflection journals
  • One-click sharing with privacy controls
  • College-ready export in seconds
📚

For Teachers

Move beyond spreadsheets. See every student's work in one place, leave rich feedback, and track progress over an entire semester without the admin burden.

  • Class dashboard with live portfolio views
  • Rubric-based feedback & annotation tools
  • Progress tracking across assignments
  • Automatic deadline reminders for students
  • Grade integration with LMS platforms
🏫

For Schools

Give every student a digital showcase that represents your institution's values. Manage curriculum standards, report to parents, and build a culture of achievement.

  • School-wide portfolio analytics
  • Standards alignment & compliance tracking
  • Parent access portal with updates
  • Custom branding & domain
  • FERPA-compliant data management

Features

Built for the real rhythm of school life

No more juggling Drive folders, email chains, and gradebooks. Profolioo brings everything together.

🎯

Standards-Aligned Tracking

Map every portfolio piece to your curriculum's learning objectives. See at a glance which standards students are meeting and where to focus next.

🔒

Privacy by Design

FERPA & COPPA compliant from day one. Students control what they share, with whom, and for how long. No data is ever sold or used for ads.

💬

Rich Feedback Tools

Leave inline comments, voice notes, or video feedback directly on student work. Feedback that sticks because it's tied to what they actually made.

🔗

LMS Integrations

Connects with Google Classroom, Canvas, Schoology, and more. Your existing workflows stay intact — Profolioo just makes them richer.

📱

Works on Any Device

Students can upload from their phone during fieldwork, or at a desktop after school. The experience is seamless on every screen size.

🚀

College-Ready Exports

One click generates a polished, shareable portfolio URL or PDF — perfect for college applications, scholarship submissions, or parent showcases.
